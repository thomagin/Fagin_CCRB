Welcome to the NYC CCRB Information Page

This page provides information and data on the Civilian Complaint Review Board (CCRB).

What is the CCRB?

The CCRB is an independent agency that investigates complaints of police misconduct.

Who is part of the board?

The board consists of 13 members appointed by the Mayor, City Council, and Police Commissioner.
